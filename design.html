design and development

The design and development team works in the same plant premises enabling an easier access and from time to time communication to the sample group, cutting room and sewing lines. With the
in-house knowledge and rich experience, the team knows how to cater to the varying requirement of different clients as well as transforming the creative concept of the clients perfectly into the end of product to maximun commercial value.

  • Technical development: the customer`s design is perfected technically in transmission into bulk production.
  • Full development: the client comes along with an idea and we do all the rest. From selecting the fabric and trimmings, developing the prototypes and appraising collection pieces to make all designs completely ready for fully factored production.
  • The aim of our marker planning is to meet and beat the rating for the best use of the fabric.
