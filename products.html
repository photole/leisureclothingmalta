products

Leisure Clothing is flexible and capable to make production in different styles as well as fabric from its two production lines---Sleeve and Bottom lines respectively where tailored jacket/blazer, coat, waistcoat, dress, jumpsuit, blouse, trousers, skirt, short, etc. are covered.  Monthly capacity from the Sleeve Line is up to 8,000 units and from the Bottom Line is up to 12,000 units. The whole production lead time from cutting to finishing is 4-6 weeks subject to the size of order and specification of model.

The U.K currently takes the bigger share of Leisure`s production capacity meanwhile the company sells the rest in other markets incl. Germany, Italy and Finland, as well as with its own collections in China.
