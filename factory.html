factory

Trade and production activities are taken place at the factory premises which is under a long term lease agreement with local government as most of other companies do in Malta. The location is at B31, Bulebel Industrial Estate, Zejtun, a ten minutes’ drive from Malta Luqa International Airport.

The size of the plants, consisting of 2 buildings with internal access to each other, is measured over 6,000 square

meters. One building houses administration offices, design & development department, cutting room and sewing lines and the other is set for pressing, finishing, warehousing, and loading bay.

Raw material, accessories are being stored at an independent warehouse, located in the same estate, and minutes away from the production plants.
